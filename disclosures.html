General Disclaimer

The information contained on this website is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal, tax, financial, or investment advice of any kind.

Visitors should consult qualified legal, tax, and financial professionals before making any investment decision. Lam Richter Corporation assumes no liability for reliance on the materials published here.

II.
Investment Risk

Real estate investments involve significant risk, including the possible loss of principal. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

  • Property values can decline due to market, economic, or environmental conditions
  • Liquidity in real estate is limited — investments may not be readily convertible to cash
  • Returns are not guaranteed and depend on numerous factors outside the firm's control
III.
No Securities Offering

Nothing on this website constitutes an offer to sell, a solicitation of an offer to buy, or a recommendation of any security or investment product.

Any offering of securities will be made only through formal offering documents prepared in accordance with applicable federal and state securities laws, which will contain important information about risks, fees, and restrictions.

IV.
Regulatory Compliance

Lam Richter Corporation operates subject to applicable federal and state regulations governing real estate investment advisory activities.
